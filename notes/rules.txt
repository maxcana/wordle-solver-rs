the 5 rules of wordle

rule #1. if the color is g, the word must have the letter in the same position
rule #2. if the color is y, the word must not have the letter in the same position
rule #3. if the color is b, the word must not have the letter in the same position
rule #4. if the colors have multiple y and g for a letter, the word must have that letter at least that many times
rule #5. if the color is b and there's a y or g for that letter, the word must have exactly y+g of that letter
